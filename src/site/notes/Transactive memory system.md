# Transactive memory system
#level2 

| **Transactive memory system (TMS)** | A collective awareness of “who knows what” within the team that facilitates information retrieval and specialization. | Lewis (2003); Kozlowski & Ilgen (2006) |
| ----------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
Intended as a [[Cognitive emergent states\|Cognitive emergent states]].
It also connects to the work of Daniela Damian in terms of awareness: 
[[Awareness\|Awareness]] is particularly important in requirements engineering because:
* the group of people who is working on common features is continually expanding and also if we consider that the team might be changing overtime if the project is particularly big, or the enterprise is changing and growing. 
* the project requirements are dynamic, and therefore it's important to know who is involved in the project and how they interact to change these requirements. According to [[damian-2007\|damian-2007]], often the requirements are influenced by [[Emergent team members\|Emergent team members]].
