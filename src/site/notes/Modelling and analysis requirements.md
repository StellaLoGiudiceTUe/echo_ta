# Modelling and analysis requirements
_Structuring requirements into organized representations (models) and analysing them for consistency, completeness, and clarity._  
Modelling supports understanding and later validation/verification.

>"My role is thus translating the product requirements into technical requirements for the units of functions that that they need to realize which, in the end altogether, then make up a good product again" - T1, Architect
