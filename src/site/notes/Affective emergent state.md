# Affective emergent state
#level1 
For example: trust and cohesion.
They enable open communication and psychological safety, therefore positively affecting team effectiveness. There is some study in which they say that trusting teams take more creative risk, this might plug into: you can more easily do problem solving, think creatively and outside of the box.
