# Achieve goals
#level2 
performance measure of team effectiveness: a team is effective (partly) when it performs well. In our case goal achievement includes delivering the system as promised or as agreed during the [[RE activities - context\|RE activities - context]]. Sometimes, goal achievement can include respecting the business and organizational constraints (time, money, resources allocated to your project).
