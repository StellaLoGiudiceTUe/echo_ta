# Evolving requirements

Requirements management is the activity of _tracking, controlling, communicating and evolving requirements throughout the lifecycle of the system._ Because requirements inevitably change, this process includes **change control, versioning, traceability and impact analysis** so that approved changes are systematically incorporated and consequences are understood. Please notice: this is mix of [[Transition processes\|Transition processes]] and [[Action processes\|Action processes]] but could be narrowed down to one of the two ==TBD==
