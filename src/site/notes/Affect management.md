# Affect management
#level2 
Managing emotional and affective aspects within the team: handling stress, emotions, interpersonal comfort, cohesion, psychological safety, such that team relationships remain constructive. Team member emotional levels can be inflated or deflated because of task conditions (failure, temporal stress), personal factors, or situational factors like job security concerns.
Techniques include: [[Provide empathy\|Provide empathy]], [[Boost team morale\|Boost team morale]], [[Control frustration levels\|Control frustration levels]].
Team activities such as [[Joking, relaxing, (openly) complaining\|Joking, relaxing, (openly) complaining]] can also be considered forms of affect management (if implemented in a manner that builds cohesion and breaks tension).
