# Conflict management
#level2 
Managing interpersonal conflicts, disagreements, and tension; handling differences in opinion, conflict resolution, negotiation to maintain functional team relations. We can distinguish two natures of conflict management: [[Preemptive conflict management\|Preemptive conflict management]] and [[Reactive conflict management\|Reactive conflict management]].
